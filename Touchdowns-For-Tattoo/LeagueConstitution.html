Section I: Meta-Rules

1. Scope and Term

This document shall govern the rules of the Touchdowns for Tattoo Fantasy Football League. It shall go into effect upon its date of ratification _________, and will be in effect every year thereafter until the Touchdowns for Tattoo Fantasy Football League dissolves.

All transactions, Matchups, and all other relevant Fantasy Football actions shall take place on ESPN’s fantasy football platform found at http://www.espn.com/fantasy/football/.

Communications of all forms (trade offers, trash talk, gifs, and general league discussion) shall remain on, but not be limited to, the Touchdowns for Tattoo Facebook Group.

In order to alter this , a vote must be initiated on an . The League Commissioner, or a , may initiate a vote on an Amendment. Once a vote is initiated, each casts a single vote on the Amendment. 11. Matchups

Each week during the Season a Team will play in a Matchup as mentioned in . During the Matchup, Teams will score points as set out in Exhibit B. The Team in the Matchup that scores the most points shall be declared the winner and awarded a “Win”. The Team in the Matchup that scores the fewest points shall be declared the loser and awarded a “Loss”.

The outcome of each Matchup will be decided on the Tuesday after the relevant week games. Stat Corrections can change the outcome of any previously concluded Matchup. In the event of a tie game, wherein both Teams score the same total points as set out in Section IV Rule 14, the “Win” will be awarded to the Team with the Bench with higher total points. In the almost impossible case that a Bench Score is also tied, each team is awarded ½ of a win. While ESPN does not track this, The will take it into consideration when creating the <.




Section IV: Game Rules

12. Rankings

Each week during the Regular Fantasy Season, all Teams shall be ranked based on their Overall Record, determined by the number of Wins each Team has. Tiebreakers concede to Teams that have more Points Scored For their Team throughout the current season.

13. Rosters

A Lineup shall consist of the following: one (1) Quarterback, two (2) Running Backs, three (3) Wide Receivers, one (1) Tight End, one (1) , and one (1) Defense/Special Teams. In addition, each Member may hold up to five (5) Players on their Bench. The sum of players on the Lineup and Bench are a team’s . The list of Players who qualify shall be updated from time to time and is available at http://www.espn.com/fantasy/football/.

2017 Addendum: There shall be zero kickers on a Team's Roster Lineup.

14. Scoring

Each week, Players shall score points based on the actions that they perform in their respective game in the National Football League. The sum of the points scored by Players in the Lineup of a respective Team each week shall be that Team’s Score for the week. A Player’s stats will be those found at http://www.nfl.com/stats/player.

Points shall be scored by Players on a weekly basis as set out in Exhibit B.

Section V: Draft Day

17. Draft Day

There is to be no holiday, save Thanksgiving, that is to be as celebrated as Draft Day. Draft Day is the Day of the calendar year in which the Members gather to Draft Players onto their Teams. All Teams must fill all roster spots during the draft.

In order for a day to be considered Draft Day there must be at least one (1) cooler full of beer, one (1) cooler full of beer alternates...but mostly extra beer. Additionally, sandwiches and other snacks must be made available including, but not limited to, buffalo wings, gaucamole, and other American snacks or snacks that America stole from other countries and made their own. For more about Draft Day History, click here.

If a Team does not have representation at the draft, they must place a best effort to Skype, Facetime, or call-in to the draft. Should a Team’s owner(s) be unable to represent their Team, Players will be selected Auto-Drafted according to ADP/Rankings of The Beer Sheet rankings.

18. Determining Draft Order

The shall determine the order of the draft picks, and shall be spun on the day of the draft. The Wheel of Destiny, shall contain sixteen (16) sections. Twelve (12) sections contain all the numbers between and including one (1) through twelve (12). The other four (4) sections are Special Sections, such as Steal a Pick, and are subject to change each year.

19. Draft Etiquette

If a Member learns of the Day of The Draft, they are obligated to inform all Members of the Touchdowns for Tattoo Fantasy Football League that intend to draft that player. They may opt to inform that Member as late as said Member’s announcement of Player selection, but must say so .
