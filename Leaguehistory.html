Founded by Kevin in 2012, Touchdowns for Tattoo is a league dedicated to Tattoo, a woman aptly nicknamed, when at the age of 80-something, she went out and got a tattoo.Fun fact: The worshipped Tattoo Trophy was created with a butterfly as the main symbol, under the assumption that she got a tattoo of a butterfly. She, in fact, did not. This history page will document many historical points, important events, and important years, of the league.
